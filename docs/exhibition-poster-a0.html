Project Overview
  • Answers in seconds — end-to-end scans feel instant on the fast local path; optional LLM path for deeper parsing of messy labels.
  • Your diet, your rules — halal, gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, nut-free, dairy-free, plus custom allergens and restrictions.
  • Snap or scan — camera / upload for any packaging; Open Food Facts when the product is in the database.

Objectives
  • Vue 3 mobile web — capture, upload, barcode, profile, history.
  • Dual-flow backend — EasyOCR + ML classifier + OCR corrector vs Mistral OCR + Mistral 7B extraction.
  • Dietary analysis — rule-based or LLM.
  • Evaluation — extraction accuracy, end-to-end latency, reliability (automated tests, high statement coverage on core services).
